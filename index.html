Brief history of Prophet Hazrat Muhammad S.A.W

hazarat Abdullh Ibn A'mro rz.a se riwayat hai, ek shakhs ne RasoolAllah ﷺ se dariyaft kiya: konsa (ahle)Islam behtar hai? Aap ﷺ ne farmaya: 'Tum khaana khelao, aashna aur Na aashna sab ko salam karo'

Hazrat Muhammad (P.B.U.H) is the epitome of compassion, kindness, and forgiveness. He is the mercy to the universe, a beacon of light in the darkness, and a guide to the righteous path. Through his teachings, he showed us the way to live a life of compassion, kindness, and forgiveness. His patience, wisdom, and courage in the face of adversity are an inspiration to millions around the world. He is the perfect example of a leader, a teacher, and a human being, and his legacy continues to shape the world today. His message of love, peace, and harmony is a guiding light for humanity. May Allah's blessings and peace be upon him, and may we strive to follow in his footsteps. He will always be remembered as a symbol of hope, faith, and devotion.
